Frequently Asked Questions

  • How important is car insurance?

    Although drivers in New Hampshire are not required to buy car insurance, it's still important to find the right policy to have a peace of mind. If anything happens, finding the right coverage will help protect you from the cost of repairs. Protect yourself and your passengers with the right coverage by contacting a licensed professional

  • Should I bundle my car insurance?

    Bundling a car insurance with a homeowners or renters insurance will help you save money. But make sure you're not losing coverage by switching to a bundle. It's best to contact RN Insurance, Inc for help.

  • What if someone is injured in my home?

    If a visitor injures themselves within your home, it's important to have the proper coverage under your home insurance. Having the right coverage means that you would be protected if the unexpected happens. An agent will help determine the right limit of liability to purchase.

  • What if my home is a total loss?

    Your homeowners insurance policy should be able to cover the full cost of building your new home. Depending on the type of disaster to your home, we recommend talking to us about the different types of coverages for your home insurance. It's important to understand that natural disasters like flood and earth-quake will need to be insured under its own policy.
